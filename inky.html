This was a really fun project. I had a very short timeline to take everything I have learned so far about UI design and create some high-fidelity wireframes for a note-taking app to be able to share the concept with shareholders. I was able to quickly come up with a name and design strategy that I was excited about and turn that into a nifty little app.

High Fidelity Wireframes

Since this was such a short timeline, I did a a few rough initial sketches then did a lot of my refining and polishing right in Figma.

Conclusion

I loved creating this project. I love a good note-taking app and I had a lot of fun playing with a limited color palette that still feels exciting. I also had a good time with the tight time constraint, because it didn't allow me to overthink and overdesign. I'm happy with the outcome.
