My Story

Hello! Thank you so much for taking the time to check out my portfolio site. My name is Janae Breunig and I am a designer. I grew up in a small town in Northwest Wisconsin before going to UW-Eau Claire where I received a BA in Graphic Design.

Since graduating in 2013, I have worked primarily in print design in many industries. I have worked on a few in-house marketing teams, as well as at a magazine where we had new projects coming up every week for various local businesses and events.

As much as I love print design, I wanted to expand my knowledge and skill base, so I have recently completed a UI Bootcamp through Career Foundry where I have learned a lot about UI design, as well as some UX process and some Front End Development. I'm so excited to continue to learn more about this field and see where these new learnings and opportunities lead.
